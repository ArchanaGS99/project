Project 4

i Phone Rating & Price Analysis

This study provides an analysis of iPhone prices and ratings, examining the relationship between the cost of various iPhone models and their corresponding consumer and expert ratings. By considering factors such as product specifications, market demand, and consumer preferences, the study aims to elucidate patterns in the pricing strategy of Apple's iPhones and the impact of these pricing strategies on consumer perception. The findings contribute to a deeper understanding of the dynamics between pricing, product quality, and consumer sentiment within the smartphone market.
